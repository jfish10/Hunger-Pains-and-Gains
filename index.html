Hi! I'm Jonathan Fisher, and you've somehow found your way onto this site - aka my personal webpage where I list all of my accomplishments and make myself look as good as possible to the outside world. The site mainly functions as a way to get to know me professionally, but with as personal a tone as I can muster. Eventually, the plan is to link to a sports/data blog that I plan on making, but in the meantime, please feel free to browse the standard info, including my resume, publications, projects, and contact info.

Currently, I'm a Projects Specialist for Lyft in the beautiful city of San Francisco, where I work with data to help keep the company's engine humming (pun definitely intended). Prior to Lyft, I was a Risk & Fraud Analyst for Ross Stores, Inc. I've come to realize that data is what will ultimately drive future changes in society (hopefully for the better), and my aspiration and goal is to be at the forefront of that change. As a result, my passion mainly lies in working with SQL, Java, Python to manipulate, transform, store, and analyze data. Extracting insights from data is simply another way of telling stories, and is a way for me to utilize my passion for living life through the stories that I create.

Thanks for visiting!
